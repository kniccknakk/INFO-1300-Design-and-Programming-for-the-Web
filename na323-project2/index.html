What is Applefest?

This weekend, the tastes and aromas of Fall goodies will perfume the Commons air as Ithacaâ€™s premiere Autumn event, Apple Harvest Festival presented by Tompkins Trust Company, makes its way to downtown Ithaca. Apple Harvest Festival draws thousands to the Commons each year.

Activities:

Open Air Apple Farmers Market on the Commons

Monday 27 September - Friday, 1 October; 12:00pm - 4:00pm

Festival food staples such as fried dough, blooming onions, apple crisps, and pumpkin funnel cake will be available.

Apple & Cider Trail around Downtown Ithaca

Monday, 27 September - Sunday 3 October; Store hours will vary

Apple & Cider Trail now available! Simply download the map, print & make your plans and let the fruitful week get started. Snap a photo of your experiences throughout the week and share them with us via Instagram or Facebook. Tag us @downtownithaca and hashtag it #FeelingAppleFestive to enter to win. No purchase necessary to participate.

Happening all week long inside shops and restaurants around downtown from Monday, 27 September - Sunday 3 October. Enjoy ciders by the glass with your meal, juice for purchase, fun apple-inspired novelties, and even sweet and savory dishes showcasing the mighty fruit!
